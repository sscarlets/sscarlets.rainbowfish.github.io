The Rainbow Fish
A stamp awaits with quiet, gleaming grace, Click once to slip into the ocean’s embrace. Dive deep where rainbow scales begin to swish, And meet the sea’s own shimmering, secret fish.
Far beneath the waves, in the heart of the vast, endless sea, there lived a fish unlike any other. The most breathtaking creature in all the ocean. His scales shimmered with every floral hue of pink, violet, and teal. Scattered among them were silver ones that caught the light like stars underwater.
The other fish couldn’t help but admire his stunning appearance and soon gave him the name Rainbow Fish. They aimed to befriend him. But he drifted by without a word, his head held high, his glittering scales catching the light as he moved on in silence.

The little blue fish had returned, gazing up at the Rainbow Fish with eyes full of longing. The Rainbow Fish hesitated, then thought, Maybe… just one tiny, glimmering scale wouldn’t be so bad. Gently, he plucked the smallest scale from his side and offered it to the little fish. The little fish tucked the shiny scale in among his plain blue ones, beaming with joy. The Rainbow Fish felt something strange stir within him — a feeling warm and unfamiliar, yet somehow... wonderful.
The End
It wasn’t long before fish gathered around the Rainbow Fish- each one hoping for a glittering scale of their own. Happily, the Rainbow Fish began to share, giving his shimmering scales away one by one. And with every scale he gave, his joy grew brighter. Soon, the water sparkled, and for the first time, the Rainbow Fish felt truly at home- surrounded by friends, no longer alone.
